Privacy Policy for Respite Driver

Last Updated: October 2025

Introduction

Respite Driver ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • What we collect: Your device's GPS location when you use the app
  • Why we collect it: To show you nearby parking areas, laybys, rest stops, and fuel stations
  • When we collect it: Only when the app is open and you've granted location permission
  • How we use it: Location data is processed locally on your device and used solely to calculate distances to nearby stops

Purchase Information

  • What we collect: Purchase and subscription data through Apple's App Store
  • Why we collect it: To manage Pro subscription access and features
  • Managed by: RevenueCat (our subscription management provider)

Usage Data

  • What we collect: App crashes, performance data, and general usage analytics
  • Why we collect it: To improve app stability and user experience
  • Managed by: Standard iOS analytics (if you've enabled this in your device settings)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription and payment processing
  • Data shared: Purchase receipts, device identifiers
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Google Maps API

  • Purpose: Route planning and Street View features (Pro users only)
  • Data shared: Location coordinates for route calculations
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple Maps

  • Purpose: Displaying maps and navigation
  • Data shared: Location data as per Apple's standard policies
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Storage and Security

  • Location data: Not stored on our servers; processed locally on your device
  • User accounts: We do not require user accounts or store personal information
  • Subscription data: Managed securely by RevenueCat and Apple

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Review what data we collect (minimal, as described above)
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Opt-out: Disable location permissions in your device settings (note: this will prevent the app from functioning)
  • Manage subscriptions: Cancel or modify your Pro subscription through the App Store

Children's Privacy

Respite Driver is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

  • Location data: Not retained; used only during active sessions
  • Subscription data: Retained by RevenueCat for as long as you have an active subscription
  • App data: Deleted when you uninstall the app

International Users

Respite Driver is designed for use in the United Kingdom. If you use the app from other countries, your data may be processed in accordance with UK and EU data protection standards.
